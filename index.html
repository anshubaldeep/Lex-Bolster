Business Valuation Untangled

Business valuation is a systematic process undertaken with an objective in mind to evaluate and appraise an entity’s overall position from financial perspective. Valuation essentially involves a set of prudent procedures employed to derive the intrinsic worth of an entity to assist in decision making.


Financial Modelling in MS Excel

Financial modelling deals with creating a mathematical model of company’s projected financials. Usually the model includes projected financial statements (P&L, BS and CFS) for next 5-7 years. These projections may be prepared for annual period or on monthly basis depending on client’s requirements.
